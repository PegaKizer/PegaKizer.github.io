History. Football(soccer) is a game that countless people love world wide, yet one must wonder when was soccer created? Now the answer to that question is fairly simple. Merly 2,000 years ago several different cultures had their own version of the game. These cultures are Mexico, China, Italy and France. Yet the soccer we know and love today was founded in England in 1863 where they formed the Football association.

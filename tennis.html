Origin. Tennis originated along the Mediterranean coast, first played by the French and Italians. Tennis gained popularity in the 11th century when French monks played the sport as pastime. Even though tennis was invented back in the 11th century the rules we know today codeified in 1874. The French gave the sport the name tennis as it deprives from the french word tenir, the direct translation means to hold. Rules. To win a point, a player must hit a shot either before the ball has bounced or after the first bounce. If the ball bounces twice, then the player with the last shot wins the point. A player must also wait for the ball to pass the net and come over to his side of the court and cannot step over the net to hit the ball.
